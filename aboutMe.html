About Me

Hello! I'm Nicholas Dueñas, a 24-year-old enthusiast of many passions, born and raised in Miami, Florida.

Whether it's on the field, the court, or the screen, my love for sports knows no bounds. As a player and avid fan, I thrive on the excitement that sports bring into my life.

My fascination with technology has been a lifelong journey. From tinkering with gadgets to exploring the latest innovations, I am constantly in awe of how technology shapes our world. Alongside my tech enthusiasm, I have a creative side that finds expression through writing poems and playing music. While I'm still honing my guitar skills, music remains a soulful escape for me.

In the gaming world, I achieved the pinnacle of success as a Grand Champion in Rocket League, a testament to my dedication and competitive spirit.

Beyond the virtual arena, I find joy in cooking and experimenting with new recipes, turning everyday ingredients into delightful culinary creations.

Environmental sustainability is a cause close to my heart. I am a strong advocate for eco-friendly practices and believe in making choices that benefit our planet.

Community service and helping others are integral to who I am. I find fulfillment in teaching and empowering others, sharing knowledge, and fostering growth. My commitment to lifelong learning and personal growth drives me to continuously seek new knowledge and experiences.

Traveling is another passion of mine, with Hawaii being the furthest and most enchanting place I've visited, leaving me with unforgettable memories.

As a nature enthusiast, I'm always on the lookout for the next great trail to explore. The serenity and beauty of the outdoors provide a perfect backdrop for reflection and adventure.

Thank you for taking the time to get to know me. I look forward to connecting and sharing more of my journey with you.
